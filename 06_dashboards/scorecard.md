# Marketing scorecard

Use only metrics stable enough to drive decisions. Directional metrics (including SOC / cost per SOC) go in the directional section with caveats. Blocked metrics stay out of the core board.

Align with [funnel_definitions.md](../01_reporting_system/funnel_definitions.md) and [metrics_readiness.md](../01_reporting_system/metrics_readiness.md). This is the CRM and reporting core of the growth-readiness system and should sit beside trusted website, GA, Meta, and channel-performance views.

One master taxonomy + one recurring scorecard template. No shadow scorecards.
