4. Early observations from CRM reporting 

Based on the July 11 reporting pull: 

- Lead volume is measurable today. 

- Source completeness remains inconsistent. 

- Campaign attribution is currently limited within available reporting. 

- Backlog and status inconsistencies make certain census-related metrics difficult to use without qualification. 

- This reinforces the importance of using Safe, Directional, and Blocked readiness labels when presenting results. 

 

One business rule that appears consistently understood across discussions is that **authorization should not be treated as active census.** 

 

For that reason, I believe marketing reporting should continue emphasizing referral volume, conversion progress, and cost per SOC rather than authorization counts.
